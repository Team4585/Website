Team 4585

Robotics should not depend on a student's ability to pay.

Husky Robotics is a student-led FIRST Robotics team at Hillcrest High School. Students build competition robots, mentor younger teams, run outreach events, and keep the program accessible for classmates across Midvale.

01

Open participation

Students can join the build, programming, outreach, media, strategy, and business sides of the team.

02

Mentorship ladder

Students learn by teaching, presenting, documenting, and helping younger FIRST teams grow.

03

Local STEM presence

The team brings robotics into schools, camps, and community settings around Midvale.

Robot and season

Designed by students. Tested on the field.

Each season starts with a new game and a blank notebook. Team 4585 turns strategy into mechanisms, code, wiring, scouting, drive practice, and a robot that has to perform under real match pressure.

  • Mechanical design and fabrication
  • Programming, wiring, controls, and testing
  • Scouting, media, outreach, and match strategy

Support the program

Giving levels

Support can be money, materials, food, mentorship, or time.
