# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Prospective students, current families, mentors, school staff, alumni, community partners, and sponsors who want to understand Husky Robotics quickly and feel invited into the team story.

## Product Purpose

The site introduces FRC Team 4585 as a student-led Hillcrest High School robotics program in Midvale, Utah. It should make the team feel real, active, ambitious, and welcoming while still giving supporters a clear path to help.

## Brand Personality

Practical, ambitious, student-centered. The voice should feel like a serious high-school engineering team with warmth, not a generic sponsor brochure.

## Anti-references

Avoid a page that feels only like a fundraising deck, a templatey all-card sponsor grid, or a vague STEM landing page with stock imagery. Avoid hiding the team itself behind donation language.

## Design Principles

- Lead with students, robots, and belonging before money.
- Use real competition and team images as evidence, not decoration.
- Make support visible but secondary to the broader team story.
- Be specific about Team 4585 history, awards, and aspirations.
- Keep calls to action useful for students, families, mentors, and sponsors.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Aim for WCAG AA contrast, clear keyboard focus, descriptive image alt text, readable mobile type, and respectful language around participation and affordability.
